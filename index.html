Back To Godhead

ETERNAL SHELTER is a platform which lets you submit an article on spirituality, which upon approval will be up on our website and you will get a good reach from here! Back to Godhead, our bi-monthly color magazine, covers all aspects of the philosophy and practice of Krishna consciousness as taught by Srila Prabhupada. Some recurring features include lectures by Srila Prabhupada, tours of Indian holy places, interviews with Hare Krishna devotees, Krishna conscious perspectives on issues of the day, excerpts from Vedic scriptures, and tips on practicing spiritual life in the modern age.
